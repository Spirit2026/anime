The B&B House Experience

Nestled on the peaceful outskirts of Tokyo, this traditional Japanese-style B&B blends natural serenity with subtle city access. The ryokan-inspired wooden home glows warmly at dusk, surrounded by a landscaped garden with a koi pond and stone pathways. This B&B also includes a Hotspring right next to the koi pond

Because this house is on the oustkirts you will be able to walk the mountains and explore the area if you want and can enjoy the wildlife and nature surrounding the house. This house also owns a zen garden for people to relax their minds and rehabilitate themselves before going back home.
